How to Add to Your Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar in a desktop web browser.
  2. On the left sidebar, click the plus sign (+) next to "Other calendars".
  3. Select "From URL" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Paste your subscription link copied above into the URL box.
  5. Click "Add calendar". Your schedule will sync within a few minutes!
  1. Open the Calendar app on your Mac, iPhone, or iPad.
  2. Go to File > New Calendar Subscription... (Mac) or tap Calendars > Add Subscription Calendar (iOS).
  3. Paste your subscription link into the Calendar URL field and click Subscribe.
  4. Set the auto-refresh interval to Every hour or Every day to receive automatic updates.
  1. Open Outlook Web or Desktop.
  2. Navigate to the Calendar view, click Add Calendar and select Subscribe from web.
  3. Paste your link and name the calendar (e.g., "XLRI ERP Schedule").
  4. Click Import.
